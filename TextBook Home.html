Welcome to TextBook.com

What is TextBook.com?

TextBook.com is the website for TextBook Studios, co-founded by BeanJean and mcsunday. It's a place to view projects, see what's coming soon and get links to helpful studios.

I'm interested in helping TextBook Studios. What now?

If you're interested in helping TextBook Studios or even just collaberating on a project then we reccomend you log onto Scratch and join the TextBook Studios studio (links can be found under the links tab at the top of the screen) and add any games/projects that you're working on or wish to collaborate on.
